# Staff SOP: Tournament Results Email

**Audience:** North Bethesda & Rockville facility staff running Link & Dink tournaments.
**When:** Within 30 minutes of the final match ending, before you leave the venue.
**Where from:** Your facility's official email address (the one on our allowlist).
**Where to:** `results@linkanddink.com`

---

## Step 1 — Take the photo (before anyone leaves)

- Gather 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place teams together on court.
- Hand them their medals/prizes so the photo shows the podium.
- Shoot **landscape orientation**, both teams centered, net or court visible in background.
- One clean photo is enough — do not send a burst or a collage.
- Confirm each player is OK being photographed for the public leaderboard. If anyone declines, skip the photo and note it in the body (Step 3).

## Step 2 — Write the subject line (most important part)

Use **exactly** this format — copy-paste the tournament slot from the schedule printout:

```
Results | <YYYY-MM-DD> | <HH:MM> | <Location> | <Bracket>
```

- `YYYY-MM-DD` — ISO date, e.g. `2026-04-11`
- `HH:MM` — 24-hour start time, e.g. `14:30` for 2:30 PM, `19:00` for 7:00 PM
- `Location` — `North Bethesda` or `Rockville` (spelled exactly like this)
- `Bracket` — the bracket string exactly as it appears on the schedule, e.g. `3.0-3.5`, `3.5-4.0 Women's Only`, `3.0-3.5 14 & Under`, `3.5-4.0 50+ Seniors`, `3.5-4.0 Mixed Gender`

**Examples**

```
Results | 2026-04-11 | 14:30 | North Bethesda | 3.0-3.5
Results | 2026-04-18 | 19:00 | North Bethesda | 3.5-4.0
Results | 2026-05-09 | 15:00 | North Bethesda | 3.0-3.5 Women's Only
Results | 2026-04-12 | 16:00 | Rockville | 3.5-4.0
Results | 2026-05-03 | 16:00 | Rockville | 3.0-3.5 14 & Under
Results | 2026-06-14 | 16:00 | Rockville | 3.0-3.5 50+ Seniors
```

If you're unsure of the exact bracket string, open the schedule on linkanddink.com and copy it from there. Do **not** paraphrase.

## Step 3 — Write the body (this exact format)

```
1st: <Player A> & <Player B>
2nd: <Player C> & <Player D>
3rd: <Player E> & <Player F>

Teams registered: <N>
Notes: <optional — ties, DQs, no-shows, singles, photo-opt-outs>
```

Rules:

- **Full names, first + last**, spelled the way they registered in CourtReserve. Check the registration list if you're unsure — getting the spelling right matters for the season leaderboard.
- Use `&` between partners, not `and` or `+`.
- If a place is vacant (e.g. only 2 teams showed up), write `3rd: —` on its own line. Do not delete the line.
- For singles events, one name per place is fine.
- Put anything unusual in `Notes:` — a manager will read it before publishing.

## Step 4 — Attach the photo

- Attach the single podium photo from Step 1. File name can be anything.
- JPG or PNG, under 10 MB.
- **Do not embed** the image in the email body — attach it as a file.
- If nobody consented to a photo, skip the attachment and write `Notes: no photo — opt-out` in the body.

## Step 5 — Send and verify

- Send from the facility email address. Personal Gmail addresses will be rejected.
- Within ~5 minutes you should get an auto-reply confirming the email was parsed:
  - **Accepted:** `<your subject line>` — you're done.
  - **Needs review:** `<reason>` — a manager will fix it, no action from you.
  - **Rejected:** `<reason>` — fix the issue and resend. Common reasons: wrong sender address, subject doesn't match the format, or a missing place line.
- If you don't get any auto-reply within 15 minutes, forward the email to a manager so it can be entered manually.

## Step 6 — Do not edit anything on the website

Don't touch the leaderboard or results page yourself — this email is the only input. If you spot something wrong on the site after the auto-reply confirms, reply to the confirmation email with the correction.

## Quick reference card (print or pin to the clipboard)

> **After the final match:**
>
> 1. Podium photo (landscape, one shot).
> 2. New email to `results@linkanddink.com` from the facility address.
> 3. Subject: `Results | YYYY-MM-DD | HH:MM | Location | Bracket`
> 4. Body: `1st:`, `2nd:`, `3rd:` lines + `Teams registered:` + `Notes:`
> 5. Attach the photo.
> 6. Wait for the Accepted auto-reply. Done.
